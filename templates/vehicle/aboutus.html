Hello DFM

The system is a web-based vehicle repair and maintenance process flow platform designed to streamline and automate the vehicle maintenance process. Objectives are such like: Efficiency: Transition from manual processes to an automated system. Accountability: Ensure transparent tracking and recording of all maintenance activities. Data Organization: Organize records sequentially per department for ease of retrieval (daily, weekly, quarterly, or annually). The Repair and Maintenance Software aims to streamline the vehicle maintenance process within the county, reducing repair costs to no more than 30% of fuel costs and improving efficiency. By implementing a clear process flow, automated record-keeping, and stage-based approvals, the system ensure that maintenance requests are managed effectively, reducing delays and unnecessary expenditures. This system automates the current manual processes and ensure systematic and sequential recording of vehicle maintenance logs.
